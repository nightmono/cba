CBA is a CSS framework for when you can't be asked to do frontend.

Classless

Works purely on semantic HTML. No CSS knowledge or classes needed.

Themeable

Different themes for different vibes.

Minimal

CBA has a minimised size of 1.6 KB. Loads blazing fast, even on slow networks.

Guide

CBA works out of the box. Just add this 1 link tag to your <head> tag and that's it!

<link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdn.jsdelivr.net/gh/nightmono/cba@main/cba.min.css">

Themes

CBA has planned themes for when you want to convey different vibes.
